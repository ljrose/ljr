The Story of This Website

 

This website was designed and constructed on and with next to nothing; no DreamWeaver, etc. - no nothing.

It was created out of desperation in a total of 4 days on a dinosaur of a computer with more disk storage than memory for the purposes of obtaining gainful employment and avoiding destitution and impending homelessness. 

It was created to attract potential employers
and those to whom the abilities demonstrated could be of use. 

It was created to demonstrate that design and technical prowess is a matter of genuine intent to communicate an idea coupled with enough intelligence, ambition, traditional/classical design experience, and talent to do so.

It was created to dispel the myth that success in art direction, usability, and product design for the web is dependent on how many URLs one has churned out for how long, the applications utilized, special features, degree of splash and flash, and name dropping.
